Edit Distance

Description

Given two strings, edit distance represents the minimum edits needed to make the two string the same. Three types of edits are available:
  1. Insertion
  2. Deletion
  3. Substitution

Example

The minimal edit distance to transform "kitten" and "sitting" is 3:
  1. kitten → sitten (substitution of "s" for "k")
  2. sitt en → sitt in (substitution of "i" for "e")
  3. sittin → sittin g (insertion of "g" at the end)
